PROTOGEAR

INDIE / AA GAME CONSOLE

VISION

ProtoGear exists to prioritize creativity and intent over spectacle. It offers a defined platform for developers and a focused, immersive space for players.

PHILOSOPHY

Simplicity, clarity, and constraint drive ProtoGear. Every system choice is made to reward thoughtful design and meaningful play.

HARDWARE SPECIFICATIONS

CPU: 6 cores / 12 threads (Zen 2 / 3)
CPU Clock: 3.0–4.0 GHz
GPU: RDNA 2 (10–12 CUs)
Compute: 2.0–2.5 TFLOPS
Memory: 4 GB DDR4-3200
Target: 900p @ 60 FPS
Storage: eMMC + NVMe
OS: BSD-based

SOFTWARE EXPERIENCE

A lightweight BSD-based OS with a custom window manager provides a stable and efficient runtime optimized for games.

DEVELOPER FOCUS

ProtoGear supports complete, replayable games, community content, and mods while maintaining a clear platform identity.

PLATFORM OBJECTIVES

  • Clear hardware constraints
  • Affordable, focused console
  • Offline-first gameplay
  • Community content and mods
  • Longevity through simplicity
