PROTOGEAR

INDIE / AA GAME CONSOLE

VISION

ProtoGear is designed to spark creativity, focusing on meaningful gaming experiences over spectacle. It offers developers a defined platform and players a space to immerse in story, challenge, and artistry, free from unnecessary distractions.

PHILOSOPHY

Simplicity, accessibility, and clarity drive the design of ProtoGear. Every detail, from hardware to runtime, is carefully crafted to reward thoughtful game design and to create an immersive, focused experience for players.

HARDWARE SPECIFICATIONS

CPU: 6 cores / 12 threads (Zen 2 / Zen 3)
CPU Clock: 3.0–3.3 GHz base, ~4.0 GHz boost
L2 Cache: 3 MB
L3 Cache: 12–16 MB
GPU: RDNA 2 (10–12 CUs)
GPU Clock: 1.6–2.0 GHz
Compute: 2.0–2.5 TFLOPS
Memory: 4 GB DDR4-3200 (dual-channel)
Performance Target: 900p @ 60 FPS
Storage: eMMC for OS, NVMe for games

SOFTWARE EXPERIENCE

A lightweight BSD-based OS with a custom window manager delivers a stable, efficient runtime. Developers interact directly with the console through the ProtoGear runtime, creating optimized experiences for both creativity and performance.

DEVELOPER FOCUS

ProtoGear encourages fully realized, thoughtful games. Cross-platform or console-exclusive titles are supported, with incentives for community content, mods, and replay value. The platform promotes meaningful creativity while maintaining system constraints.

PLATFORM OBJECTIVES

  • Deliver a stable, developer-focused platform with clear constraints.
  • Provide an affordable console with a curated game library.
  • Encourage creativity and thoughtful design within defined limits.
  • Focus on immersive offline-first gameplay with optional online functionality.
  • Support community content, mods, and replay value.
